Our Mission

CrowdWise is an enterprise software company that provides automated strategic consulting services to medium and large organizations. Powered by the wisdom of
the crowd, our platform produces cheaper, quicker, and far more accurate strategic solutions than ever before.

We provide our clients with a platform that incentivizes their employees to suggest projects, make predictions, or offer new solutions. The clients' internal pool of employees invests in the best ideas through crowdfunding mechanisms. They then receive comprehensive strategic solutions, powered by the people who know the organization the best.

About Us

We started CrowdWise while studying at Yale University. The company was founded based on our passion for large-scale collaborative thinking processes that harness the wisdom of the crowd for superior results.
