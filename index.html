About us

CrowdWise was founded by two Yale researchers based on their passion for large-scale collaborative thinking processes and the theory of Collective Intelligence. The company provides automated open innovation services to different industries.

Why choose us?

Powered by the wisdom of the crowd, the CrowdWise program produces cheaper, faster, and far more accurate strategic solutions than ever before. We provide our clients with a platform that incentivizes their most precious stakeholders (e.g. employees, customers, contractors, etc.) to suggest projects, make predictions, and offer new solutions. The client's internal crowd invests in the best ideas through crowdfunding mechanisms. Our client then receives comprehensive strategic solutions, powered by the people who know the organization the best.
